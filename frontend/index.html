Tools we use

We design presentations applying PowerPoint, Google Slides, Prezi, and Daze,
our own HTML presentation-making service.

Daze allows creating presentations in the form of a webpage. You can use it as a document, an embedded presentation on your website, or even make a separate landing page based on your slide deck. With Daze, a usual webpage functionality, such as hyperlinks, animations, videos, is available for you.

As for now, Daze Presentations can be ordered from us, as well as all other Types of Presentations. Later we will launch a self-service tool, so you will be able to create stunning presentations by yourself.
